How BarterRoot Works

Trade fairly using our value point system

⚖️ Standard Value Units (SVU) — Point Rates

🌾 Grains (Rice, Wheat, Dal)50 SVU / kg
🍎 Fruits120 SVU / kg
🥦 Vegetables40 SVU / kg
🥛 Dairy80 SVU / kg
💊 Pharmaceuticals800 SVU / unit
📦 Other goods60 SVU / kg
1

📝 List your goods

Add what you want to give. SVU points are calculated automatically from category and quantity.

2

🔍 Get matched

The platform finds users whose goods have equal SVU value to yours — fair exchange guaranteed.

3

🤝 Send trade request

Review the SVU breakdown, accept or negotiate. Both must agree before exchange starts.

4

🚚 Exchange goods

Meet locally, use delivery, or go via warehouse hub. Confirm receipt when goods arrive.

5

⭐ Rate your partner

After exchange, rate the trader. Builds trust for the whole community.

Example: Ravi has 5 kg Rice = 250 SVU. Priya has 2 kg Apples = 240 SVU. Difference is only 10 SVU (4%) — within fair limit. They exchange. No money needed! ✅
